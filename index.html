Trip Planner With Earth In Mind

Next year I will embark on a year long world trip. Being somewhat environmentally conscious, I want to leave a small carbon footprint behind. Therefore, I want to know which itinerary with which mode of transportation is the best for my journey and how big my total carbon footprint would be.

This project displays in a an interactive manner the difference in CO2 output and km travelled of different trips.
